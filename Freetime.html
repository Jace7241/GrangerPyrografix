A Welcome Note From the Artist

Ever since I can remember I’ve loved drawing and painting. It was something that I greatly enjoyed and am very passionate about.

I'm an avid member of the NAHS (National Art Honor Society), and I also enjoy taking the time to make beautiful works of art. When I was little I would meticulously paint pictures with my fingers and proudly hang them on the fridge. As I grew up my artistic taste and talent did too. Every moment I was drawing or painting something. I would be painting a wooden forest one minute and inking a picture of a camera the next.

At some point I wanted to branch out my talent and experiment with different art forms. I went from paintings and ink drawings to polygraphic and abstract computer graphic vectors and pieces. I also traded in my ink pen for a wood burning pencil.

With this, I created elaborate pyrography pieces of art, that of which would spark art commissions I realized that I couldn’t just stay with one art form. Drawing, painting, wood burning, and creating vectors were all a part of me that I would cherish. It was that moment when I wanted to expand and touch a larger audience with my art.

With the help of my sister, Zoe (who did most of the work), together, we launched this website to reach my goals.
